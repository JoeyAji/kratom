Green Kratom

Green Kratom is scientifically in the same classification as coffee. Rich with Flavonoids and Alkaloids the Green Vein Kratom is known as a naturally occurring hybrid of White Vein and Red Vein Kratom. There are 13 different types of Green Kratom and it is one of the most popular types of Kratom.

Green Plantation Maeng Da

Green Planation Maeng is in the same family as Green Mango Da, however it is grown in fields compared to Green Mango Da which is grown in the wild. It is potent in alkaloid content and it also has a rich green color from the actual green leaf which produced the powder.

Green Kapuas

Another type of Green Kratom Green Kapuas is produced in Malaysia and is quickly gaining popularity. It is rare but highly regarded and it’s also known as Hulu Kapuas.  It is a white/green strain with slightly higher than average energetic properties.

Green Horn

Green Horn Kratom Leaves are some of the rarest and high quality around. The name comes from the shape of the leaf compared to most other forms of Kratom. It is native to South East Asia but has quickly spread across the globe. It is very highly regarded in the Kratom community as one of the most clean looking Kratom leaves on the market.

Super Green Malay

Super Green Malay is known to be one of the most effective forms of Kratom. The term Super does not refer to superiority over other strains of Kratom. However, it is known as “Super” because of the way it is harvested. It involves plucking strictly massive leaves for it’s harvesting.

Green Thai

Green Thai is another distinction of Green Kratom and it is named after it’s native country of Thailand. It grows everywhere in the country of thai land and can also be found in other South-Eastern Asian countries. The leaf that produces Green Thai is a rich green colored plant.

Green Mango Da

Green Mango Da is known to be the most popular Kratom strain. It has an intense smell and the “green” aspect of this particular distinction is due to the green color of the leaf which is grinded up. It is a sub-division of Maeng Da Kratom and is known for it’s calm and peaceful nature. Green Mango Da is regarded as one of the highest rated forms of Kratom.

Green Malay

Green Malay is named after it’s native country Malaysia. It is known for being one of the most medicinal strains of Kratom. It is grown on the Malaysian mountains and this environment adds to the longevity of the Kratom it produces. The color green also comes from the vein of the leaf in which produces it.

Red Kratom

All forms Red Kratom are known to come from leaves that have a reddish, green color and have been becoming more and more popular amongst users. The different color comes from a chemical differential in the tree which produces the leaves themselves. This is usually due to things like climate and harvesting techniques. Red Veins are found in many varieties of Kratom.
